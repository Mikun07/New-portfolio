# Non-Functional Requirements

**Project:** Ayomikun Festus-Olaleye Portfolio
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Date:** 2026-06-28

---

**NFR-001 - Performance**
The portfolio shall achieve a Lighthouse Performance score of ≥ 90 on desktop. The production JS bundle shall not exceed 400 KB gzipped.

**NFR-002 - Responsiveness**
The layout shall be fully usable on viewports from 320 px (mobile) to 1920 px (wide desktop). The top navigation shall remain usable on desktop and collapse into a mobile drawer below the `md` breakpoint.

**NFR-003 - Accessibility**
All interactive elements shall be keyboard-navigable. All images shall have descriptive `alt` attributes. Colour contrast ratios shall meet WCAG 2.1 AA for text against background tokens.

**NFR-004 - Internationalisation**
The system shall support 5 display languages (EN, DE, FR, NL, SV) with no untranslated strings visible to the user. Language preference shall persist across sessions via `localStorage`.

**NFR-005 - Theme**
Dark and light modes shall be supported. The active theme shall persist across sessions via `localStorage`. The system shall respect the user's OS `prefers-color-scheme` on first visit.

**NFR-006 - Reliability**
The contact form shall show an error toast if the EmailJS send fails, ensuring the user is informed of any delivery problem.

**NFR-007 - Maintainability**
TypeScript strict mode shall be enforced (`"strict": true` in `tsconfig.json`). ESLint shall pass with zero warnings at `--max-warnings 0`. All translation keys shall be type-safe - adding a key to `en.ts` without updating all locale files causes a compile error.

**NFR-008 - Testability**
Form reducer logic shall be unit-tested in isolation with Vitest. The test suite shall pass with 6/6 assertions on every CI run.

**NFR-009 - Deployability**
The application shall build successfully with `npm run build` producing a `dist/` output deployable to Netlify. Secrets shall be injected as environment variables; none shall be hardcoded in source.

**NFR-010 - Security**
No sensitive credentials (EmailJS keys) shall be committed to source control. All external links shall include `rel="noreferrer"` to prevent tab-napping. Form inputs are validated by HTML5 `required` attributes and type constraints.
