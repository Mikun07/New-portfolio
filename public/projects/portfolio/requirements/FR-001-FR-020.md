# Functional Requirements

**Project:** Ayomikun Festus-Olaleye Portfolio
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Date:** 2026-06-28

---

## Home Section

**FR-001** - The system shall display the user's full name, professional title, and location on the Home section.

**FR-002** - The system shall provide a "Get in touch" CTA that smooth-scrolls to the Contact section.

**FR-003** - The system shall provide a "View experience" CTA that smooth-scrolls to the Experience section.

**FR-004** - The system shall provide a "Download CV" link that downloads the résumé PDF.

**FR-005** - The system shall display a core technology stack as a list of pill badges.

## About Section

**FR-006** - The system shall display a skills breakdown organised by category (Languages, Frontend, Testing, Backend, Tools, Software Engineering, AI/ML).

**FR-007** - The system shall display education entries with degree, institution, location, period, and detail.

**FR-008** - The system shall display certifications as clickable links that open the PDF in a new tab.

## Experience Section

**FR-009** - The system shall display work experience as a vertical timeline with role, company, location, period, employment type, and bullet-point achievements.

## Services Section

**FR-010** - The system shall display 6 service offerings, each with an icon, title, description, and tech tags.

## Projects Section

**FR-011** - The system shall display 6 projects in a responsive grid (3-col desktop, 2-col tablet, 1-col mobile).

**FR-012** - Each project card shall display a title, subtitle badge, truncatable description with "Read more / Show less" toggle, tech tags, and links to the GitHub repository and live site (where applicable).

**FR-013** - The system shall visually distinguish one project as "Featured".

## Contact Section

**FR-014** - The system shall display contact details (email, phone, location) with clickable email and phone links.

**FR-015** - The system shall provide a contact form with fields: name, email, subject, and message.

**FR-016** - The system shall send the form data via EmailJS on submission and display a success or error toast notification.

**FR-017** - The system shall reset all form fields after a successful submission.

**FR-018** - The system shall disable the submit button while a message is being sent.

## Global / Cross-Cutting

**FR-019** - The system shall support toggling between light and dark colour themes, persisted to localStorage.

**FR-020** - The system shall support switching between 5 display languages (English, German, French, Dutch, Swedish), persisted to localStorage.
